Volunteer

Volunteering your services to help others is not just hugely satisfying — it can teach you skills that are invaluable to potential employers. Joining our team is an opportunity to continue your professional development, and we offer access to free training in return for your time.

Alternatively, you may be interested in joining the Management Team or becoming a Trustee? Let us know when you’re free and what skills you have to share — contact Poppy, our Arts Centre Manager, on 07932 772905.

Donate

100% of public donations go towards funding free arts activities for local children in need in the school holidays — a lifeline for parents needing urgent respite. It costs £69 per day per child, including breakfast and a hot meal, to sponsor a child to attend.
