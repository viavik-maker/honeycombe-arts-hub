Artist Support & the Access Grant

Helping young and emerging creatives take their next step — into training, opportunities and the creative industries.

We’re putting the finishing touches to this

The Access Grant is part of how Honeycombe Arts Hub supports young and emerging creatives. We’re finalising the details now — if you’d like to be first to hear when applications open, register your interest.

What is the Access Grant?

Honeycombe Arts Hub exists to open doors into the arts — especially for young people who face barriers of cost, circumstance or confidence. The Access Grant is designed to remove some of those barriers, giving young and emerging creatives practical support to keep developing their practice and find their route into the creative industries.

Who it’s for

Young and emerging creatives connected to the Hub and the wider Boscombe community. [Exact eligibility — ages, area and stage of practice — to be confirmed.]

What it could support

Depending on the final scheme, an Access Grant might help with things like:

  • Materials, equipment or tools to make new work
  • Short courses, workshops or one-to-one mentoring
  • Building a portfolio or showing work to an audience
  • Travel or other costs that make an opportunity possible

These are the kinds of things the grant is intended to help with — the final categories and any amounts are still being confirmed.

How to apply

Applications aren’t open just yet. When the scheme launches we’ll publish who can apply, what’s available and how the process works, right here. For now, the best thing to do is get in touch and we’ll let you know the moment it goes live.
